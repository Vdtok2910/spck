INTERNATIONAL DAY OF HAPPINESS

-20th March 2023-

March 20th is the UN International Day of Happiness.

We can create a happier and kinder world together by adopting a simple, daily practice.

TRY THIS NOW

STEP 1

Relax your breathing and notice:

How am I feeling right now?

STEP 2

Look around you and ask yourself:

What am I thankful for?

STEP 3

With warm feelings, ask yourself:

Who do I want to send love to?
